Welcome!

Thanks for helping us try out a brand new style of password scheme. When you hit Start, you'll be present with three random passwords, one at a time. Memorize them as best you can! You'll get the chance to practice entering each one before moving to the next one.

Once you've memorized your passwords, you'll be asked to enter them one at a time, in a random order. You'll get three tries to enter each one.

And that's it! Press Start whenever you're ready to begin.
